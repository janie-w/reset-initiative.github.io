ABOUT SCI ENG TECH

Reinventing Education in Science, Engineering, & Technology

Our goal: see increased STEM & STEAM engagement among minority youth by reaching them in the classroom during the school day

By introducing underpriviledged kids to the fascinating world of science, engineering, and technology, we hope to show them that they're capable of enjoying and being successful in these highly employable fields and thus creating a brighter future both for themselves and for America.

We're a nonprofit run by students, for students. Based out of Loudoun Academy of Science, we've been working to make a difference since 2014.

Science.

We're delivering experience-based learning to the elementary school classroom with hands-on, in-school demos and entertaining videos with guided experiments that are specifically crafted with the fifth grade brain in mind. By allowing students to learn by doing instead of by reading out of a textbook or watching a lecture, we're making science fun and interesting again.

Engineering.

We offer an engineering competition to allow students to learn about and experience the cycle of trying and failing until finally finding a solution that is ultimately the heart of engineering. Students get to learn valuable skills such as persistance and problem solving and experience the joy of a hard-earned success all while engaging in healthy, fun competition with their peers.

Technology.

To address the lack of technology education in elementary schools, we're giving fun coding workshops to allow students to get their feet wet with computer science. By introducing them to these topics at a young age, we hope to make coding seem more accessible and less intimidating. This way, they can see that coding is something they can do, as well as that it can be fun and useful.
